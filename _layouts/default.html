Shriya T.P. Gupta

Hi! I am Shriya, currently working as a Software Engineer at Microsoft. I recently graduated with a B.Eng. in Computer Science from BITS Pilani Goa Campus, India.

I completed my bachelor thesis in the Augmented Vision department of the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI) in Kaiserslautern, Germany. I worked under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Didier Stricker on the topic of 3D human pose estimation using RGB and LiDAR for the use-case of autonomous driving.

My research interests broadly span the intersections of artificial intelligence and computational neuroscience, focusing on deep learning based visual perception tasks.
