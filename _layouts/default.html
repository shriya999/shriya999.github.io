Shriya T.P. Gupta


Hi ! I'm currently working at Microsoft R&D and I graduated with a B.Eng. in Computer Science from BITS Pilani, India.

Previously, I did my undergraduate thesis in the Augmented Vision department of the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI) in Kaiserslautern, Germany. I worked under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Didier Stricker on the topic of 3D human pose estimation using RGB and LiDAR for the use-case of autonomous driving.

My research interests broadly span the intersections of machine learning and cognitive neuroscience, focusing on deep learning based visual perception tasks.
